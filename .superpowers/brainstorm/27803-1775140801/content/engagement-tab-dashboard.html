The Killer Feature: Section-Level Scroll Depth

Scroll depth maps to your structured content — Because we know the page structure (hero, topic 1-5, summary, sources), we can track exactly which sections people read and where they drop off. This is unique to ConvexPress — GA4 alone can't tell you "68% of readers made it to Topic 3."
Actionable insight — If Topic 4 has a huge drop-off, you know to rewrite or move it. If the CTA has a 23% click rate, you know it's working. This turns the editor into a data-driven content optimization tool.
Built-in tracking powers this — Scroll depth and click events are tracked natively via a lightweight script on the website frontend. No GA4 needed for this — it works on every ConvexPress site from day one.
